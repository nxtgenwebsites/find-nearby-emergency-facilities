HealthCentreApp Partnership Models

Collaborate with Us to Expand Access, Empower Citizens & Strengthen Health Systems.

HealthCentreApp offers a range of partnership models tailored for individuals, communities, organisations, and institutions that share our mission of delivering digital health access across Africa. We believe health transformation is most powerful when it’s collaborative — whether you're a healthcare professional, content creator, NGO, sponsor, or policymaker, there’s a way to partner with us.

Referral Partners
Help us grow our reach by inviting others to register for the Free Edition.Ideal for individuals, influencers, and community leaders who want to:
  • Promote digital health adoption across their network
  • Support others in accessing modern healthcare services
  • Earn rewards and build long-term income through our structured referral programs

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.

Silver Monetisation Program (SMP)
Available to all subscribed users.
  • Earn 25% of every subscription fee from users you invite
  • Earn 25% of the revenue from Sponsored Ads you create and publish
  • Automatically included for all DigitalHealth Standard subscribers

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.

Gold Monetisation Program (GMP)
Available to Premium Edition subscribers who complete the Certified Digital Health Practitioner (CdHP) training.
  • Earn 50% of every subscription fee from users you invite
  • Earn 50% of the revenue from Sponsored Ads you create and publish
  • Completion of CdHP training automatically upgrades your account to Community Edition, allowing you to manage and support up to 50 local users
  • To maintain GMP eligibility, the CdHP must have exactly 50 supported community members registered under their Community Edition account

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.

Certified Digital Health Practitioners (CdHPs)
CdHPs are trained Premium users who support their local community through the Community Edition of HealthCentreApp. They act as trusted digital health advocates for citizens who may not own smartphones or understand how to use digital tools
  • Protect health data through confidentiality, privacy, and safeguarding best practices
  • Support up to 50 local citizens in accessing digital health tools and triage services
  • Help users create and manage CareRequests and CareRecords
  • Earn recognition and income as Community Digital Health Leaders

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.

Partnerships with Care Providers
Partnering with HealthCentreApp enables hospitals, clinics, and private practitioners to deliver mobile-enabled electronic patient records (EPR) and participate in a citizen-first digital health ecosystem. Through this partnership, care providers can act as Certified Digital Health Practitioners (CdHPs) and register all their patients under a unified, secure, and portable record system. HealthCentreApp ensures that data remains patient-owned but seamlessly accessible to authorised care providers, enhancing continuity of care and clinical efficiency.
  • Access a mobile-ready, cloud-based patient record platform without large infrastructure costs
  • Improve patient retention through personalised digital engagement and continuity of care
  • Reduce administrative workload with digitised triage, record-sharing, and consent management
  • Strengthen compliance with data privacy and health information governance standards
  • Access customised configurations to match specific clinical workflows or operational needs
  • Participate in national and regional digital health initiatives through scalable integration options
  • Enhance reputation as a digitally enabled healthcare provider aligned with future health standards

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.

Diaspora and Philanthropic Gifting
Diaspora members and philanthropists can give back by gifting digital health subscriptions to loved ones or communities. Through our GiftedMedical vouchers, you can:
  • Instantly send Standard or Premium Edition subscriptions to others
  • Support healthcare and well-being instead of sending cash
  • Track voucher usage and receive updates through CareRemote (with recipient consent)

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.

Corporate and NGO Partnerships
Ideal for organisations seeking to make measurable impact through health sponsorships and community engagement. Corporate and NGO partners can:
  • Sponsor bulk subscriptions of Standard or Premium Editions through GiftedMedical vouchers
  • Run community health campaigns and outreach programmes
  • Align with CSR and ESG objectives through measurable health impact
  • Monitor programme outcomes through voucher tracking, usage analytics, and anonymised dashboards

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.

National, State, and Regional Partnerships
We collaborate with government and institutional partners to customise and localise HealthCentreApp for specific countries or regions. We can:
  • White-label the platform with national or regional branding
  • Tailor functionalities to align with local health systems and policies
  • Support deployment for national health strategies, digital inclusion programmes, or emergency health response systems

Contact Us to learn more about becoming a Referral Partner.
