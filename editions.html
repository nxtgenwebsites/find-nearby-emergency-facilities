Introducing HealthCentreApp

Your digital health companion â€” Built for access. Powered by purpose.

HealthCentreApp is a citizen-first digital platform designed to make healthcare accessible, efficient, and secure, anywhere, anytime. Whether you live in a bustling city or a remote village, it helps you find nearby medical services, organise your health data, and connect with medical professionals locally and globally.

The app starts with a Free Edition available worldwide and offers additional subscription editions for users who want advanced digital health tools. Together, these editions empower individuals, families, communities, and national health systems.

From the Free Edition, users can also:

  • Create Sponsored Ads to promote services or local businesses.
  • Buy GiftedMedical vouchers to gift any subscribed edition to others.

Our subscription editions also enable users to monetise their account through our partnership programs.

Monetisation Programs
Monetisation Programs
SMP (Silver Monetisation Program)
  • Available to all subscribed users.
  • Earn 25% of every subscription fee from users you invite.
  • Earn 25% of the revenue from Sponsored Ads you create and publish.
  • Automatically included for DigitalHealth Standard subscribers.
GMP (Gold Monetisation Program)
  • Available to Premium subscribers who complete the Certified Digital Health Practitioner (CdHP) training.
  • Earn 50% of every subscription fee from users you invite.
  • Earn 50% of the revenue from Sponsored Ads you create and publish.
  • Completion of CdHP training automatically upgrades your account to the Community Edition, enabling you to manage and support up to 50 local users.
  • To qualify for GMP, Premium users must also register and support at least 50 local users under their Community Edition account.

Digital Health Community Edition
(Subscription)
The Community Edition is a free upgrade available exclusively to Premium subscribers who complete the Certified Digital Health Practitioner (CdHP) training. It enables trained practitioners to manage and support their registered community members through the HealthCentreApp platform.
  • Free Upgrade : Automatically activated for Premium users who achieve CdHP certification.
  • CdHP Support : Provides guided help and health assistance to community users.
  • Full Premium Tools : Includes all Premium Edition features for supported users.
  • Community Access : Each CdHP can support up to 50 adults under their care.
  • Bridging the Digital Divide : Ensures citizens with limited digital skills can still benefit from digital healthcare through guided support.
National Health Edition
